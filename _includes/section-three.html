What We Offer

  • Summer Camps

    INTech Scholars participate in a 5-day experience where they learn how to build websites. They are placed in teams in which they choose a social issue to focus on for their final product. In addition, Scholars network with women in the industry and participate in company tours.

  • One-Day Camps

    INTech Scholars spend a day engaging in our 3 Pillars, spending the majority of their time producing a technological product that revolves around a social issue of their choice.

  • Mini-Camps

    A 1-hour experience where women in the industry teach participants about technology and facilitate a CS-Unplugged activity in the classroom.

  • High School Club

    Stay tuned as we plan to pilot a High School Club for girls where they dedicate time after school to learn industry level technology platforms.
